How to play " DAG "
Rules of the Game:

• Before starting the game, players select an ecuation. Remember that each equation is of different difficulty levels. Players then choose characters and settings to create a story that corresponds to the equation. Don't forget that you can give the character any name you like.

• The game has three diffrent levels. Your goal when you begin is to select or create the most creative and accurate story compared to other players.

• You can seek asistance from your teacher during the game.

• Writing the story in a clear and explanatory manner is crucial. Good strategy and creative thinking will help you earn points and win.
